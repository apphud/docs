Amplitude

Amplitude is a powerful mobile analytics service. Send events to Amplitude via Apphud, analyze your users behavior and make necessary desicions.

How to Add Integration?

  1. Open Amplitude and sign in.
  2. Click on "Manage Data" at the bottom of the screen:

  1. Choose your app:

  1. Copy Amplitude API Key:

  1. At Apphud go to "Integrations" section and add Amplitude:

  1. Enter Amplitude API Key at the "Amplitude API Key" field:

  1. You can enter your custom event names or disable some:

  1. Tick "Enable this integration":

  1. Save changes:
