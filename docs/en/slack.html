Slack

Receive events about subscriptions to Slack.

How to add integration?

  1. Add incoming webhook in your Slack account.
  2. Copy given Webhook URL.
  3. At Apphud go to "Integrations" and add Slack:

  1. Insert Webhook URL at "Webhook" field. Enter username which will be shown in notifications and channel name:

  1. You can disable events you don't need:

  1. Make sure "Enable this integration" is checked:

  1. Save changes:
